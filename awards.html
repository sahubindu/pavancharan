Awards And Achivements

As Playback Singer in film industry, Surya pavan charan's voice enthralls listeners from around the world. He has received many
awards including "NANDI AWARD-2018" from State Government as BEST MALE SINGER for MAA T.V. seethamaalakshmi title song.
"UGADHI PURASKAR - 2016"from Delhi telugu academy, Delhi.
"GHANTASALA PURASKAR" from State of Andhra Pradesh, "UGADHI PURASKAR - 2013" from kala sudha Telugu foundation, Chennai.
YOUNG ACHIEVER award from Madras Telugu Academy , Chennai 2003. Recipient ofNational Talent Scholarship from C.C.R.T., New Delhi from 2000 to 2005.

He received many titles from prestigious organizations including Kala saradhi, Little roses , Vizianagaram Brothers, Sivasankari pavan charan etc..

Pavan Won Gold Medal in the state level Annamacharya songs competition organized by Smt. Sobharaj through Annamacharya Bhavana vahini, Hyderabad.

Pavan has performed a range of concerts & Festivals worldwide. He has B High grade in Carnatic Music from prasar bharati (AIR) & doordarshan.

Given many Music concerts & stage shows in prestigious TLCA(Telugu literacy and cultural association).

Many shows conducted by TANA & ATA of United States of America along with Music director Manisharma Team.
